CloudNineMC Rules

Rule 1 - Hacking and Alts

Hacking in any form, with any modded/edited client is disallowed.

Optifine, Lunar and Badlion Clients are allowed. All features of these cleints are allowed.

The term “hacking” means any add-on to the Minecraft client that allows you to do things that are not in the “vanilla” game.

Alt accounts are not allowed to be used to avoid bans, join other towns and destroy them from the inside, etc. Basically no alts for malicious purposes.

Rule 2 - Griefing

Griefing is not allowed in claimed areas but is allowed in unclaimed areas. If the build is a hate symbol please report it to staff by making a ticket in the discord.

Building obnoxious things in a 200 block radius of a claim is not allowed.

TNT, Obsidian buildings or anything that could potentially harm town players is also not allowed within a 200 block radius without permission.

You also cannot make cobble terraformed by lava and water anywhere on the map.

You are not allowed inside another town, so for example, joining a town just to take it down. This means no stealing, completely destroying or doing it to get information on them. This will be a 10 day ban.

To add on to this, if you kick anyone from your town you must let them retrieve their belongings before they leave.

You are not allowed to claim around someones base in an attempt to stop them from doing anything, this is not allowed in any circumstances

Making a town near a player just to stop them from claiming anymore is also not allowed

Rule 3 - Chat (Applies in discord as well)

Swearing is not allowed.

Don’t ask for personal information. This will get you banned as this falls under the doxing rule.

Spamming is not allowed at any point.

Advertising is not permitted on the server, please do not advertise sexual websites that may include things children should not see. We have a younger player base meaning they are more vulnerable to these things then the older. No ip-grabbing or phishing websites used to grab personal information about someone and no malware that could potentially destroy someone's computer.

Trash talking is allowed but do not take it too far, if you are getting too toxic you will be banned or muted depending on the severity

Racial abuse or homophobia/use of racist slang/phrases is not allowed at any point. This WILL be a permanent ban.

Rule 4 - Exploits

The use of any exploit on the server is not permitted; this includes but is not limited to, duping, teleport glitches, invincibility, money glitches, any form of exploit involving books. All forms of book based duplication exploitation is bannable. The use of books in a process known as “book-banning” is also disallowed and bannable in all forms. We understand that players enjoy writing a book or two, but symbol filled and banknote rich literature is not permitted.

Exploiting the shop, or abusing glitches in the shop, is not permitted.

Rule 5 - Crashing

Trying to crash the server through any form external or internal is not allowed, for example, DDosing, lag machines, null pings.

Rule 6 - Farming

Certain farms such as: 0 tick, Lag producing machines are not allowed. Anything else intending to cause lag or interfere with the servers tps will be destroyed rightfully by staff.

Auto clickers are not allowed.

Rule 7 - PvP

TP killing is not allowed.

Trapping is also not allowed. For example, using doors to keep someone inside of your claim to PvP them and then loot them. Try to keep PvP outdoors.

TP killing could be if you get a friend to tp to a player, you tp to your friend and kill the player. Normal TP killing/trapping would be teleporting to someone, or requesting for someone to teleport to you, and then killing them through any means.

Do not /sethome in other people's towns / claims.

Rule 8 - Threatening to hack or exploit

If you threaten to hack or exploit on the server we will take action.

Rule 9 - Claims

If you happen to not claim something that is your own fault and not be to staff responsibility please do not blame anyone else for your own regards.

Vehicles that are left ANYWHERE will be removed by staff to clear lag, please make sure to pick up your vehicles after you are finished with them.

Rule 10 - Toxicity

Being toxic to players in chat and in the game is not allowed! If you are being toxic to anyone will result in either a mute or ban depending on the severity of the toxicity. Toxicity includes but is not limited to:

  • Sexism
  • Harassment
  • Discrimination against over players
  • Hate Speech
  • Encouraging self harm or saying something intending to harm other people.
  • Or genuinely being toxic to other people.

Doxxing, threatening to dox, or sending doxes, this includes but is not limited to, sending IP addresses, addresses, pictures. (Doxing is a serious offence)

The Agreement

By playing on the server and being a member of the discord you agree to these rules and that we have the right to mute, kick or even ban you if you do not comply with these rules, if you do not agree with the rules outlined here or do not understand one or more of them please immediately message one of the staff team. We also have the right to alter these rules at any time if we see an issue with them! We are not required to announce when we update the rules, you must check these before doing anything that may be considered against the rules.

If you would like to make any changes to these rules you may on our GitHub page: https://github.com/GoldenEdit/cloudnineweb
